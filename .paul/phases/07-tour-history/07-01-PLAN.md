<objective>
## Goal
Add a dedicated tour history page showing past refill tours: who refilled, when, which machines, and what products were added. Enhance the activity log metadata written during tours to enable this view.

## Purpose
Operators need visibility into past refill tours for accountability, planning, and identifying patterns. Currently, tour data is scattered as individual `stock_refill_tour` entries in the activity log without product-level detail or tour grouping.

## Output
- Enhanced metadata in refill tour activity log entries (tour_id for grouping, per-product breakdown)
- New `useTourHistory` composable for fetching and grouping tour data
- New `/tour-history` page with grouped tour cards showing operator, timestamp, machines, and product details
</objective>
